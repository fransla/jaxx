ShapeShift
x

ShapeShift lets you convert (or "Shift") between the all of the coins/tokens offered by Jaxx. Enter the amount you want to convert (either in fiat or the coin currently being displayed), then, using the toggle button just below the Amount field, select what coin type you would like to convert to.

At the bottom of the ShapeShift display, you'll see the current conversion rate and the minimum and maximum transaction size allowed by ShapeShift.

Once you've made your selection, select Shift. This will bring you to a confirmation screen where you can review and complete the transaction.

Please note that ShapeShift transactions may take up to 10-20 minutes to appear in the target coin's wallet screen.

Help
x
How do I send and receive funds?
To send funds from Jaxx, select the Send button. In the first box, enter your recipient's address, or select the QR icon in the text field to scan their QR code. In the second box, enter the amount of bitcoin/ether you want to send. If you wish to enter the amount in fiat currency, select the dollar symbol on the left. Select Send.

Jaxx offers two methods to receive funds: scan the sender's QR code with the camera function, or generate a QR code requesting a specific amount by entering the amount and selecting Generate.
How do I transfer my DAO Tokens?
As of Jaxx 1.0.1, you can Send and Receive DAO tokens via the main DAO screen - select The DAO icon from the top of the Jaxx screen. Please note that if you have committed tokens to a Vote using another service, the Tokens associated with that account will be "locked" until the Voting period has concluded.
How can I convert between coins/tokens?
As of Jaxx 1.0.2, Jaxx integrates the blockchain conversion service ShapeShift to enable in-app conversion. Select the fox icon below the Amount bar on any wallet screen to view the ShapeShift options. A toggle will appear with the options for that wallet screen (i.e. BTC -> ETH). Enter the amount you want to convert in the Amount field, wait for the Shift button to activate, then select it.
How do I back up Jaxx?
Jaxx offers users a 12-word backup phrase to back up and restore your funds, private keys, and transaction history. Go to Tools > Display Backup Phrase to view and verify your backup phrase. We recommend writing down an exact copy of this phrase and keeping it in a secure location.
How long will it take to receive funds after they've been sent?
In most cases, funds will appear in your Jaxx within a minute or two. To update your balance, select the Refresh button above your balance. If the funds are being processed by an external service (eg ShapeShift), they may take longer to appear.
Does it cost anything to send and receive funds?
Jaxx is completely free to download and use on all platforms. However, the networks that administer your funds (i.e. the Bitcoin network and the Ethereum network) will charge a small processing fee per transaction to ensure that the transaction goes smoothly, and to reward miners. Jaxx itself takes no commission.
What's an HD wallet, and why does it change my address?
HD stands for "Hierarchical Deterministic." HD wallets generate a new address every time funds are received to your current address. This helps to keep your funds private. All previous addresses can be reused to receive funds and add to your Jaxx balance.

The standard applies to all coins/token wallets.
How do I sync Jaxx across devices?
From the Pair Devices menu, select Display Pairing Code. This will display both the QR code and the backup phrase associated with your wallet. Use the other device to scan the QR code (mobile/tablet only), or enter the backup phrase manually.
What happens if I lose my secure backup phrase?
It is vital that you write down and store your wallet's backup phrase in a safe location, as it is the only way you can restore your entire Jaxx wallet, should you need to.
Do I need internet access to use Jaxx?
In order to send funds and refresh your balance, you will need to be connected to the internet. However, you can take a screenshot of your QR code to enable users to send funds to your wallet whether or not you are online.
What devices can I access my Jaxx on?
Jaxx is available for most phones and tablets. It is also available as a desktop application for PC, Mac, and Linux, as well as an extension for Chrome and Firefox. You can pair your wallet across all devices.
Can I use unconfirmed funds?
Yes, you can use the funds in your Jaxx before they are displayed as Confirmed.
What does Transfer Paper Wallet do?
Transfer Paper Wallet adds the funds from an existing private key into Jaxx by scanning its QR code or entering it in text form. This will not overwrite your existing wallet, just add the funds associated with that key to your balance.
How do I add a security PIN?
In the Settings section, select Setup Security PIN. Enter a 4-digit PIN, then re-enter to confirm. This PIN will now need to be entered whenever the Send function is used, and also when a user attempts to access the wallet-pairing tools or private keys.
How do I reset my wallet?
The procedure to reset your wallet varies between devices. See more information here.
What if I need more support?
Head to jaxx.io and see our support page to let us know about your specific problem and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
